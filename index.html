'Ped Charity

Charity 'Ped

The motorcycle club is a charity based organization out of Seattle Academy of Arts and Sciences. We will collecting left over food from our cafeteria in addition to getting larger organizations such as Safeway, QFC, Walmart, and other grocery stores to donate leftovers. We will be riding these mopeds from grocery store to grocery store and picking up food. Then we will transport these items to food banks and soup kitchens to help support the local homeless population.



Ridin' the 'Ped

Ridin'

We will have 'peds for a few founding members that have licenses but the majority of the gang will be on bicycles. This is a liability issue but if you are interested in the charity or engineering in the club, we believe that the biking will be a fun activity as a group.

Buildin' the 'Ped

Buidlin'

The final aspect of our club is engineering. With our faculty representative, Tom Flood's expertise in building and fixing mopeds we will build our own moped. This moped will be worked on by all members of the club and it will be called the Charity Chopper. This moped will be used to carry food to shelters and food banks. We hope to engage the members of our club while we commit good deeds.
